\question
Consider the experiment in which a coin is tossed repeatedly until a head
comes up. Describe the sample space. \\
\solution
Let's denote the outcome of the experiment by a random variable $Y$ such that
it maps to the following set of integer $Y \in \cbrak{0,1,2,\dots}$, where
$Y = i$ denotes that numbers of tails that occured before a heads came up is $i$
such that $i \in \cbrak{0,1,2,\dots}$. \\
By $Y$, The sample space $S$ of the experiment can be shown as:
\begin{align}
	S = \cbrak{H, TH, TTH, TTTH, TTTTH\dots}
\end{align}
